Board differences that matter for the camera

BoardCPUCamera connector
Pi 3 B/B+Cortex-A53, 1.4 GHz1× 15-pin CSI (standard)
Pi 4 BCortex-A72, 1.5 GHz1× 15-pin CSI (standard)
Pi 5Cortex-A76, 2.4 GHz2× smaller CSI/DSI, own cable

A Pi 3/4 camera ribbon will not fit a Pi 5 without an adapter — this trips up more beginners than the wiring itself.
